Instructions

-> Once the timer starts, you have 5 seconds to memorize the shapes (and their order) from the top frame.

-> After the timer stops and the top shapes are hidden, press on a shape from the bottom frame.

-> If the shape and its color match the one you memorized, it will dissapear. Otherwise you will lose a live.

-> If you remembered all the shapes in the right order, you will get to the next level.

-> If you lose all the lives, it's Game Over.

-> Try to get to the highest level you can.

-> Good Luck!
